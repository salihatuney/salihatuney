### About me

I'm a Senior Data & AI Engineer with 7.5+ years of experience, currently building **enterprise GenAI platforms in air-gapped environments** within the aerospace & defense sector. My work sits at the intersection of LLM orchestration, data engineering, and identity management — all behind firewalls with zero internet access.

I also run **BlackBox Labs**, a freelance AI consultancy focused on secure, on-premises AI solutions.

📍 Eskişehir, Turkey

### What I build

🔒 **Air-gapped LLM platforms** — Deploying vLLM, RAG pipelines, and AI assistants in closed networks where `pip install` is a luxury  
🔍 **RAG systems** — Hybrid search with Weaviate + bge-m3, reranking, evaluation with DeepEval & Langfuse  
🎤 **AI transcription** — WhisperX-based meeting transcription with speaker diarization on NVIDIA RTX A4000  
🛡️ **Enterprise SSO/RBAC** — Keycloak integration with AI platforms (Dify, custom apps), JWT-based authorization  
📊 **Data engineering** — PostgreSQL warehousing, Apache Hop ETL, SAP CDS views, OpenMetadata governance
